About Me

My name is Nicholas Roberto and up to this point the majority of my working career has revolved around film. I developed a love for cinema as a child moving all over the globe due to my dad's Air Force job. As soon as I entered high school I began working on local independent films and after acrueing the necessary experience I was granted union membership to IATSE as a set electrician. In between set jobs I also worked for a company restoring vintage movie theaters and eventually opened an online business selling rare and out of print DVDs that I often sourced while traveling for work. Outside of film some of my interests include just about anything physical or outdoors (hiking, biking, weight lifting, climbing, obstacle races, etc.), spending time with my 2 year old daughter Nora, cooking, reading, and beer festivals. My decision to shift careers to programming came shortly after my daughter was born and I realized that I would need to make some big changes to support my family. I spent some time trying to learn on my own and was excited about learning a skill that has an endless amount of application and creative possibility but hit a wall with self-study. Shortly after, I discovered Epicodus and i'm excited to continue with the program and see where it leads me.
